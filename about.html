About GhaBhi Foundation

Creating sustainable solutions for people and the planet.

Our Story

GhaBhi Foundation was established to inspire environmental responsibility and improve the quality of life in communities through innovative sustainability programs. We work with volunteers, schools, local leaders and development partners to create cleaner, healthier, and greener environments.

Vision

To become Africa's leading environmental NGO by empowering communities to build a sustainable future through innovation, education and collective action.

Mission

To promote environmental sustainability through climate action, renewable energy, sanitation initiatives, education and community partnerships.

Core Values

Integrity

We remain transparent and accountable in every action.

Innovation

We embrace creative ideas that solve environmental challenges.

Teamwork

Collaboration is the foundation of our impact.

Sustainability

Every project is designed for long-term positive change.

Why We Exist

Climate change, pollution, poor sanitation and environmental degradation continue to affect millions of people. EcoRise Foundation exists to provide practical solutions through education, advocacy, innovation and direct community action.
